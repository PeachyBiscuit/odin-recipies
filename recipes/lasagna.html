Lasagna Recipe

This homemade lasagna recipe is delicous and easy to make. It features layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese with a golden cheesy topping!

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the ground beef and cook for 6-8 minutes, until browned and cooked through.
  2. Add the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the lasagna is cooked through and the cheese is melted.
  3. Serve the lasagna on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or plastic wrap. Top with the remaining mozzarella cheese and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.

Enjoy!
