Brownie Recipe

This simple brownie recipe is perfect for breakfast, snacking, or even a dessert. It's a delicious and filling treat that's easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup packed dark chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, sugar, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the egg and milk, stirring until combined.
  4. Gradually add the heavy cream, chocolate chips, cocoa powder, and vanilla extract, stirring constantly until smooth.
  5. Scoop the dough onto a lightly floured baking sheet, making sure it covers the entire surface.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool the brownies on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Enjoy your chewy brownies!
  9. Remember to refrigerate the brownies for at least 2 hours before serving.
